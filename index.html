Zalouk Recipe


Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants
  • 2-4 large tomatoes
  • 1-2 cloves of garlic
  • 1-2 teaspoons of ground cumin
  • 1-2 teaspoons of paprika
  • 1-2 teaspoons of cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1-2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Instructions

    1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).

    2. Wash the eggplants and tomatoes.

    3. Cut the eggplants in half lengthwise and score the flesh with a knife.

    4. Drizzle with olive oil and roast in the oven for about 30-40 minutes until soft.

    5. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté minced garlic until fragrant.

    6. Add chopped tomatoes, cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.

    7. Cook until tomatoes are soft and sauce thickens.

    8. Once eggplants are roasted, scoop out the flesh and add it to the tomato mixture.

    9. Mash everything together and cook for an additional 5-10 minutes.

    10. Serve warm or at room temperature with bread or as a side dish.
